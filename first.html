About KVK

KVK is one of the largest applied sciences universities in Lithuania. It is located in the beautiful port city of Klaipėda.

The university offers 6 faculties and 16 study programs in the fields of engineering, business, health sciences, and social sciences.

KVK has a strong focus on practical training and research, with many opportunities for students to gain hands-on experience in their chosen fields.

The university has a diverse student body, with students from all over Lithuania and many other countries. It also has a strong international focus, with many opportunities for students to study abroad and participate in international research projects.

Overall, KVK is a great choice for students who are looking for a high-quality education in a historical and vibrant city.
